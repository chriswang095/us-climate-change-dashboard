Raw datasets can be downloaded from the following locations:
  1. EV Infrastructure: Cleaned version available in the folder. Source: US DOE Alternative Fuels Data Center https://afdc.energy.gov/files/docs/historical-station-counts.xlsx?year=2023
  2. GHG Emissions: US EPA Greenhouse Gas Inventory Data Explorer https://cfpub.epa.gov/ghgdata/inventoryexplorer/#allsectors/allsectors/allgas/econsect/all
  3. Vehicle Registrations: US DOE Alternative Fuels Data Center https://afdc.energy.gov/vehicle-registration?year=2016
  4. ZEV Sales: Aggregated version available in the folder. Source: US DOE Alternative Fuels Data Center, Various manufacturer sales reports
